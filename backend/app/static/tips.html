Speaking Mastery Tips

Effective strategies to boost your confidence and fluency.

1. The Shadowing Technique

Listen to a native speaker and repeat exactly what they say at the same time. This helps with rhythm, stress, and intonation.

2. Record & Review

Always use SpeakBetter's recording feature! Listening to your own voice is the fastest way to spot repetitive errors and filler words.

3. Think in English

Instead of translating from Hindi in your head, try naming objects around you and describing your actions in English throughout the day.

4. Don't Fear Mistakes

Fluency is more important than perfect grammar. Focus on getting your message across first; the accuracy will come with practice.
